Yoga Classes

Gentle Hartha Yoga

Intended for beginners and anyione wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture), pranayma (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.

Vinyasa Yoga

Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-sychronized movement- you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses.

Restorative Yoga

This 90 minute class features very slow movements and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. this calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate injury
